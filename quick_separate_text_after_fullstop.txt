quickly separate the whole trascript with full stops.
1. copy the whole transcript.
2. open ms word, take a new blank document.
3. paste speacial as text only.
4. enter ctrl + f ( to open find menu)
5. click on right side drop down in navingation pane.
6. goto replace tab.
7. give the .(fullstop) in find what area.
8. give the .(fullstop) in replace with area and click on more.
9. click on special at the bottom.
10. choose manual line break.
11. click on replace all.
12. click yes in the pop up.
13. now the whole text will be separated with a enter after the fullstop.
14. copy the whole text and paste it in your recording instr subsheet.
