get automatic slide numbers in ppt.
1. open the marking or animation ppt.
2. go to view tab.
3. select slide master.
4. choose the blank layout.
5. at the bottom right corner we'll have a placeholder for page number like (#) in a rectangle box.
6. click on that rectangle box, goto shape format.
7. click on eidt shape which is in insert shapes group.
8. choose the Oval shape.
9. then in the size group give the Height as - 0.91cm, and width 2.76cm.
10. then click on shape fill give the blue color for image insert instructions template, green for marking template.
11. enter " A " left to the (#) symbol inside the ovel, then change the text color to white.
12. go to insert tab, click on Header & footer, tick the slide nuber check box, then click on Apply to all.
13. then change the layout to the same one which you have created in slide master.
